Expert Veterinary Care in the Comfort of Your Home

At Grand Ave. Pet Hospital, we understand that some pets feel anxious or stressed when visiting a clinic. For pets who prefer the comfort of home, we offer professional house visits, ensuring they receive quality veterinary care in a familiar environment.

Why Choose a House Visit?

🏡 Reduces Stress & Anxiety – Perfect for nervous pets or those who dislike travel
🐾 Ideal for Senior Pets & Mobility Issues – No need to transport pets with arthritis or other mobility concerns
🚗 Convenience for Busy Pet Owners – No more scheduling conflicts or long wait times
🩺 Personalized One-on-One Attention – Tailored care in a relaxed setting

Our House Call Veterinary Services

Our mobile veterinary team provides a range of services right at your doorstep, including:

✔️ Wellness Exams & Preventative Care – Regular checkups, vaccinations, and health assessments
✔️ Pet Vaccinations – Essential immunizations to keep your pet protected
✔️ Microchipping – Quick and painless identification for your pet’s safety
✔️ Senior Pet Care & Pain Management – Specialized treatment for aging pets
✔️ Euthanasia & End-of-Life Care – Providing compassionate goodbyes in a peaceful home setting
✔️ Minor Illness Treatments – Care for non-emergency conditions like ear infections, skin issues, and allergies

How to Schedule a House Visit?

📅 Booking a house call is easy! Simply call us or request an appointment online. We will arrange a convenient time to visit your pet at home.
